WELCOME TO GOLD’S NATIONAL SPELLING BEE

Events Ghana Consult in collaboration with the Ghana education Service, The Ministry of Education, and The Student Enhancement Program, Ghana, is humbled by the opportunity to introduce to your highly esteemed educational institution, an opportunity to participate in this year’s Spelling Bee Competition, dubbed the GOLD’S NATIONAL SPELLING BEE COMPETITION.

Forword

First and foremost, we seek to congratulate your highly esteemed institution, on meeting the selection criteria. And as such, we are always humbled with the opportunity to work with your institution towards attaining the same aim

SELECTION CRITERIA

Based on a very keen search, our coordinators nationwide managed to put likewise institutions in a bracket. Here are a few tips we considered during selection

  1. Credible institution, with recognition from the Ghana Education Service.
  2. Must fall within the Top 10 bracket at the district level.
  3. Must have participated in similar competitions, either locally or internationally.
